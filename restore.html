Restore Rebalance

Sound Healer Branding

Brief_

The client for this brief being a sound healer was looking to brand themselves. The practice was centered around Tibetan Singing Bowls which is all to do with balancing the frequencies of our body. The client wanted to give themselves credability through the design, whilst holding trates such as calmness, trust and relaxation.

Response_

A lot of branding within this area seemed to be quite similar and I felt it was important to give the client something different. I decided to encorporate the Tibetan Singing bowl within the word mark to give it significance while also being an attractive motif.
