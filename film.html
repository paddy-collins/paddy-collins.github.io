Film Titles

Art direction

Brief_

For this project I was aksed to create the introductory title sequence for a movie named 'I am not a monster'. Along with the titles sequence itself I was asked to create some promotional pieces such as posters and a website. The title sequence was to convey emotion and distress to se the scene.

Response_

I decided to take an abstract approach when trying to convey these feelings. I started videoing ink spills on paper and got some interesting results. When I started making music to match up to the videos I knew I was close to what I wanted to achieve, and from this point I kept reshooting until I was happy.
